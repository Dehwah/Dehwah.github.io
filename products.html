Main products

The Powermote and the dual GPS-accelerometer mobile traffic sensor are based on the same high performance ARM Cortex M4 processor, which can be dynamically configured in the 8-168 MHz range. The Powermote is adapted to high performance, solar powered sensing systems (both for smart cities and industrial monitoring). The dual GPS-accelerometer mobile traffic sensor is particularly adapted to high accuracy traffic sensing applications, operating in conjunction with short range wireless sensor networks.
