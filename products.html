Main products

Our dual flood/traffic sensor and its associated gateway are based on the high performance ARM Cortex M4 processor, which can be dynamically configured in the 8-168 MHz range. Both systems can be equipped with a variety of wireless transceivers, and are solar-powered for easier, faster and more economical deployments. With a remote connection capability, the software of both systems can be remotely updated as new versions become available.
