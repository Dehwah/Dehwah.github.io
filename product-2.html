Our backend gateway is fully compatible with our flood sensor system, and based on the same ARM Cortex M4 processor. Solar powered and wireless, it can receive the data generated by a sensor network of flood sensors and upload to the cloud using a variety of networking interfaces. The gateway has USB, Ethernet and Wireless interfaces.

The gateway can be configured to upload measurement data to the cloud, enabling real-time flood and traffic monitoring applications.
