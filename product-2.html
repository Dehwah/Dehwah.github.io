The dual GPS-accelerometer traffic sensor can fuse GPS data with accelerometer data in real time through Extended Kalman Filtering to generate traffic data of unprecedented quality and accuracy. In addition to monitoring traffic congestion, this sensor unit can monitor the condition of roads in real time using its built-in 3D accelerometer.

This sensor unit is compatible with a wide variety of short to medium range transceivers, and can operate in conjunction with the Powermote.
